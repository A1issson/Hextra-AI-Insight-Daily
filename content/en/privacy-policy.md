### Tracking Technologies & Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep an eye on activity on our Service and stash away certain info. The tracking technologies we deploy include beacons, tags, and scripts. Their job? To collect and track info, helping us level up and analyze our Service. The tech we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a small file dropped onto your Device. You can totally tell your browser to reject all Cookies or give you a heads-up when one's being sent. Heads up though, if you don't play ball with Cookies, you might miss out on some parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to say "no thanks!" to Cookies, our Service might just use 'em.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might contain tiny electronic files called Web Beacons (aka clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These bad boys let the Company (for example) count users who've visited those pages or opened an email. They're also used for other related website stats, like noting how popular a certain section is or verifying system and server integrity. Sneaky, huh? 😉

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies chill on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser. Poof! 💨

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for these purposes:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely crucial for hooking you up with services available through the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They help authenticate users and prevent sketchy, fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for simply can't be delivered, and we only use these Cookies to give you those services. No joke! 💯

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are here to figure out if users have given the green light to the use of Cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're chilling on the Website, like recalling your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every single time you use the Website. Sweet, right? 🤩

For more info on the Cookies we use and your options concerning them, pop over to our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
